This dataset presents a global monthly paleo-reanalysis of the modern era from 1421 to 2008. It supports large-scale climate variability studies and long-term historical climate analysis.

**Coverage:** Global, 1421–2008  
**Variables:** temperature, precipitation, SLP, wind speed   
**Methods:** Paleo-reanalysis integrating observations and model-based reconstruction  
**Related publication:** [ModE-RA: a global monthly paleo-reanalysis of the modern era 1421 to 2008](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-023-02733-8)  
**Access:** [MoDE-RA](https://www.wdc-climate.de/ui/entry?acronym=ModE-RA_info)
