HCLIM is a global historical climate database designed to support climate reconstruction, reanalysis, and the study of historical climate variability.

**Coverage:** Global  
**Variables:** Air-temperature, precipitation and pressure
**Methods:** Database compilation and harmonization of historical climate records  
**Related publication:** [The global historical climate database HCLIM](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-022-01919-w)  
**Access:** [HCLIM](https://doi.pangaea.de/10.1594/PANGAEA.940724)
