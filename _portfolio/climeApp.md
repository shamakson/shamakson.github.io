ClimeApp is an interactive data processing and visualization tool for the ModE-RA global climate reanalysis. It allows users to explore anomalies, composites, correlations, and source data for monthly climate fields.

The tool was created by Niklaus Bartlome and Richard Warren, with co-development by Noémie Wellinger and Tanja Falasca. I contributed through review and testing of the web application and am a co-author of the related ModE-RA data paper.

**Coverage:** Global, 1422–2008  
**Variables:** Temperature, precipitation, sea level pressure, Z500    
**Functions:** Anomalies, composites, SEA, correlation, source exploration    
**Tool:** [Open ClimeApp](https://mode-ra.unibe.ch/climeapp/)    
**Related publication:** [ClimeApp paper](https://doi.org/10.5194/cp-20-2645-2024)
