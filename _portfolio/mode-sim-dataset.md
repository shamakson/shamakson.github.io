This data product provides access to a subset of ModE-Sim variables, forcings, boundary conditions, and example run scripts. The dataset supports climate variability studies during the modern era and is connected to the ModE-Sim ensemble framework.

**Type:** Climate model data product  
**Coverage:** Modern Era simulations  
**Contents:** Selected variables, forcings, boundary conditions, and example run scripts  
**Main archive:** [World Data Center for Climate: ModE-Sim](https://www.wdc-climate.de/ui/entry?acronym=ModE-Sim)  
**Individual experiment sets:**  
- [Set 1420-1](https://doi.org/10.26050/WDCC/ModE-Sim_s14201)  
- [Set 1420-2](https://doi.org/10.26050/WDCC/ModE-Sim_s14202)  
- [Set 1420-3](https://doi.org/10.26050/WDCC/ModE-Sim_s14203)  
- [Set 1850-1](https://doi.org/10.26050/WDCC/ModE-Sim_s18501)  
- [Set 1850-2](https://doi.org/10.26050/WDCC/ModE-Sim_s18502)  

**Related publication:** Hand, R., Samakinwa, E., Lipfert, L., and Brönnimann, S. (2023). "ModE-Sim – a medium-sized atmospheric general circulation model (AGCM) ensemble to study climate variability during the modern era (1420 to 2009)." *Geoscientific Model Development*.  
**Access note:** Further variables are available upon request by contacting the authors.
