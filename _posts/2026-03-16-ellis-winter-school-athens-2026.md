I attended the [ELLIS Winter School co-organised by Unit Jena in Athens](https://ellis.eu/events/ellis-winter-school-at-unit-jena-athens-2026), held at the National Technical University of Athens from 16–20 March 2026.

The winter school focused on AI for Earth system science, hazards, and climate extremes, bringing together researchers and practitioners working at the interface of artificial intelligence and climate science.
