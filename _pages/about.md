I am a climate scientist working on paleoclimate variability, hydroclimate extremes, ocean circulation, and climate data reconstruction.

My research combines climate dynamics, numerical modeling, statistical analysis, and data-driven methods to investigate climate variability across seasonal to centennial timescales.

This website contains my publications, talks, teaching, datasets, and blog posts.
