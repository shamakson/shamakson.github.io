I am a climate scientist working on climate variability, climate extremes, ocean circulation, and climate data reconstruction. My research combines climate dynamics, statistical methods, reanalysis products, and numerical modeling to better understand variability across seasonal to centennial timescales.

My recent work has focused on global monthly sea surface temperature and sea ice reconstructions, paleo-reanalysis products, Atlantic jet stream variability, climate extreme events, and climate data tools for analysis and visualization.

I am particularly interested in:

  • climate variability across timescales
  • climate extreme events and public health
  • climate reanalysis and reconstruction
  • climate data analysis
  • climate services via data products, tools, and reproducible workflows
